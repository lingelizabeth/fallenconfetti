About me

Hi! I’m a sophomore at Harvard studying Computer Science. I use technology to create societal change, especially through Machine Learning research in healthcare or data analytics for nonprofits. I love building an idea from scratch to a functional product, and you can view my side projects here. I tell stories through design. Good design empowers technical products to reach and understand their audience. Without it, the project lacks essential ingredients: usability, addressing the right problem, a narrative. This semester, I’m Chief Design Officer at Harvard’s flagship Data Analytics Group. I’m currently based in Watertown, MA. When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me cooking pasta with my roommates, exploring Cambridge and Boston, or making spreads for my bullet journal.
